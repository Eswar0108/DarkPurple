The Problem

What's slowing your restaurant down?

⏳

Long Wait Times

Customers wait 5-10 min just to get a waiter's attention, browse the menu, and place an order. Peak hours = frustrated customers.

📋

Paper Menu Limitations

Can't update prices or items instantly. Reprinting menus costs money. Worn menus look unprofessional.

❌

Order Errors

Miscommunication between customer → waiter → kitchen. Wrong items served = wasted food & unhappy customers.

📊

No Visibility

No way to track what's popular, which tables need attention, or how fast orders are being served.

The Solution

Scan. Browse. Order. Done.

Each table gets a QR code. Customers scan it with their phone — no app needed. The full menu loads instantly. They order directly. Kitchen gets it in real-time.
